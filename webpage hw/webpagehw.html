What is this place?

This website was designed to illustrate the correlations between 500 cities' latitudes and the effects this had on their climate. On this site, one will be able to see various graphs depicting the raw data and correlations (or lack their of) between aspects that were chosen that commonly are associated with a region's climate. However, this is not all that is provided. The raw data is also available if one wanted to dig deeper into the topic, or believed there is a misrepesentation of the data through the graphs.
